Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

The short version: this tool collects nothing. Every password is generated entirely inside your own browser and is never sent anywhere.

What we collect

Nothing. No accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, no tracking pixels, no cookies, no third-party scripts or services, and no collection of any personal information. Passwords are generated entirely in your browser and are never sent to a server, because there is no server.

The only data stored on your device

The optional Save history feature, off by default, keeps your recent passwords in your own browser's local storage. This data never leaves your browser, is never sent to us or anyone else, and can be permanently deleted at any time with the Clear button in the History section. If you never turn it on, nothing is stored at all.

Changes and contact

If these practices ever change (for example, if advertising is added later), this policy and the "Last updated" date above will be updated. Questions? Open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
